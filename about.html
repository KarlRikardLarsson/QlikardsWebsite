Hi, I'm Rikard.

Business Intelligence consultant at Kolibrivision in Stockholm, Qlik enthusiast all the time. Most people online know me as Qlikard — a name that kind of chose itself.

I've spent years working with Qlik across different industries and clients, and I've noticed the same thing everywhere: good knowledge is hard to find. It's buried in forum threads, scattered across PDFs, or locked inside someone's head. This site is my attempt to fix that — at least for the things I keep coming back to.

What you'll find here

  • Scripts — Load script patterns, data modeling, QVD best practices
  • Visualizations — Chart design, set analysis, expressions that actually work
  • Qlik Automate — Step-by-step automation flows and integrations
  • General — Everything else that makes Qlik a little less painful

Who this is for

Whether you're a developer deep in the load script, an analyst trying to get a chart to behave, or someone who just got handed a Qlik project — there's something here for you. I write the posts I wish I could have found when I was stuck.

Got a question or a topic you'd like me to cover? Reach me at rikard.larsson@kolibrivision.se
